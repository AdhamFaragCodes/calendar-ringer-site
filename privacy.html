Privacy Policy – Calendar Ringer

Effective Date: September 18, 2025

Calendar Ringer (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we access, how we use it, and how you can control your data.

Information We Access

  • Google Calendar events: We request the scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.evenets.readonly to read your upcoming events (start/end times, title, and Google Meet links) so we can show reminders and provide a “Join” button.
  • Basic profile: We request openid, email, and profile to identify which Google account is connected and display your account email inside the extension.

How We Use Information

  • Event data is used only to calculate reminder times and to display a Google Meet join link.
  • Profile data is used only to show which account is connected. It is not used for marketing or shared with anyone.

Data Storage and Retention

  • All data is processed locally in your browser.
  • Data is stored in memory only and is discarded after use or when you close your browser.
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers.

User Control

  • You can disconnect at any time which revokes our access and clears any cached data.
  • You can revoke access directly in your Google Account security settings.

Data Sharing and Human Access

No human has access to your data. We do not share, sell, or transfer data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Calendar Ringer is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will appear at this URL with a new effective date.
