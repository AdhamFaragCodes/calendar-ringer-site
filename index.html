Calendar Ringer

Never miss a Google Meet again. Calendar Ringer gives you a friendly popup and ringtone a few minutes before each meeting so you can join on time.

Why users love it

  • Custom ringtone that plays until you join or snooze.
  • One-click join buttons for Google Meet or the original calendar event.
  • Works entirely locally – your calendar data never leaves Chrome.

Get started

Install Calendar Ringer from the Chrome Web Store, sign in with Google, and choose how many minutes of heads-up you want. That’s it!
